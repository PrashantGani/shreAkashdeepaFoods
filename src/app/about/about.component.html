Our Story

Food is the lifeline of a human being. Without food, no living being can survive, whether it is a plant or an animal. It is a known fact that food is the source of energy for all. However, only humans have countless choices in everything, including food. Humans desire to consume food in various forms. Even if it is a single vegetable or grain, they enjoy it in hundreds of different names and preparations.

Our company, "Shri Akashadeepa Food Private Limited," brings together a blend of uniqueness, systematic cleanliness, and excellence in food preparation. We cook and serve meals that bring happiness to both the mind and heart of our customers. For the past five years, we have been offering continuous service to our customers with this goal in mind.

The beginning of our company itself is an interesting story. In our family, which consisted of six members — two parents and four children — we were leading a difficult life without capital and struggling to make ends meet. Amidst managing household expenses, nurturing children, and their education, our lives took an unexpected turn overnight due to a small incident that left us empty-handed.

The result of that incident was the creation of "Mane Aduge Halli Uta" (Home-Cooked Village Meal). Starting with this name, Shri Akashadeepa Food Private Limited has grown steadily and has now become a successful enterprise. Today, we directly provide employment to around 25 people and indirectly to more than 10 others. Through this growth, our business has brought us financial stability and prosperity.

I am forever indebted to the COVID-19 pandemic (Corona 19) for being the turning point that allowed us to rebuild and reshape our lives. I always remember those days and strive to protect and grow our present life. I offer my gratitude and prayers to nature, God, the divine, and our customers for blessing us with this opportunity and support.

Our Team

Catering Services

Shri Akashadeepa Food Private Limited is proud to offer high-quality catering services for all kinds of events, including weddings, corporate gatherings, birthday celebrations, and more. Our catering team specializes in creating customized menus tailored to your preferences and requirements.

We emphasize fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and unparalleled service to make your event truly memorable. Whether you need a simple buffet or an elaborate multi-course meal, we ensure that every dish meets our high standards of quality and taste.
